Awards and recognition

  1. In 2010, Wipro Lighting won Design for Asia award in the product design category for its LED streetlights.
  2. In 2016, Wipro Lighting won India Design Mark for Certified Excellence in Product Design.
  3. In 2017, Wipro Lighting won International Diamond Prize for Excellence in Quality by European Society of Quality Research.[48] In the same year, it won, Red Dot Design award for outstanding design, Global SSL Showcase Top100, India Design Mark for certified excellence in Product Design, and Frost & Sullivan award for LED lighting visionary innovation leadership.
  4. In 2019 Wipro 3D won Frost & Sullivan Automotive Additive Engineering and Manufacturing award
